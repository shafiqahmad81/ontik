About Us

Ontik Began in 2016 With a Clear Vision: to Transform Bold Ideas into Real Outcomes.

Ontik was founded in 2016 with a clear vision: to bring bold ideas into scalable ventures. We began with technology services to understand the market and build strong execution capabilities.

Over time, this foundation enabled Ontik to evolve into a full-fledged venture studio, combining venture management, strategic advisory services, and leadership development. Today, we build and scale creative, advisory, and product-driven companies under one integrated ecosystem
